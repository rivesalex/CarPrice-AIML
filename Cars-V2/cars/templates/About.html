About

I am a first generation immigrant. Originally, I was born in Cuba, and immigrated to the United States when I was still at a pretty young age. As I matured, I found myself enjoying tasks that required a lot of thinking and a lot consideration of possible solutions and since I was mechanically inclined, this led me to pursue a degree in Mechanical Engineering. 

My degree has allowed me to experience multiple different domains, and I have gained perspectives at multiple different stages of a company/organization. All the way from research and development in the medical industry to supply chain management in the automotive industry, to modeling and analyzing spacecraft in the space industry. 

What I currently do is definitely a departure from the expectations I had when I decided to become a mechanical engineer (it is more focused on data analysis and data science). However, much of the engineering intuition, methodologies, and curiosity has carried over in a very beneficial way.
